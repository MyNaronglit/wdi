Story

Wichien Dynamic Industry has, for more than 40 years, been making ceaseless efforts to deliver to our customers’ quality standards. Our continuous dedication has resulted in specialized designs and manufacturing of various automotive lights for OEMs, agricultural, construction, and commercial vehicles. We make a wide product range including headlamps, tail lamps, driving lamps, work lamps, LED lamps, and many other car accessories. With our increasing expertise in advanced automotive, heavy duty machinery and mining lighting, we have leveraged our experience to expand into general lighting products. The latest product line is established under brand Faclite. The products have been well received by many customers for both indoor and outdoor applications such as factories, warehouses. billboard producers, sport stadiums, office buildings, churches and temples. We are proud to deliver premium quality lighting with superior energy efficiency and long life to the public.
